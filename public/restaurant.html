The Restaurant

Come and enjoy the taste of the authentic italian food experienced at the distinctly appointed with Angelic & Lion Head flowing fountains & Italian hand-painted mosaic tile tables.

The Lounge

Our famous lounge boasts an exclusive Italian-style courtyard, dubbed the "Corta Bella" with wood beamed ceilings & artistic treasures that would make Michelangelo smile.

The Patio

Our distinctly appointed with Angelic & Lion Heads, bubbling water pools and flowing fountains offers an exquisite dining experience for a your memorable social or private event.

The Bar

The jewel of the restaurant, our renowned bar has been the watering hole for many celebrities, industry leaders and business executives in the Bay Area for many years.

The Cellar

Our private brick vaulted Wine Cellar, well stocked with award-wining wine selection form around the world, can seat 25 guests comfortably. It is ideal for family gatherings or business functions.
